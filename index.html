About me

I'm currently an assistant professor at School of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU).
I am also a member of the Information Security and Intelligence Cognition Lab (ICL), working closely with Prof. Xinghao Jiang and Tanfeng Sun.

Before joining SJTU, I worked closely with IEEE Fellow Prof. Hong Yan and Min Xie as a postdoctoral research fellow at the City University of Hong Kong (CityU-HK) (2021.12-2024.02). I was also an associate researcher at the City University of Hong Kong Shenzhen Research Institute in (2021), and a visiting Ph.D. student with the Rapid Rich Object Search Lab (ROSE) led by IEEE Fellow Prof. Alex C. Kot, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore (2019-2020). I received a Ph.D. degree in Cybersecurity from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU,2021), under the supervision of Prof. Xinghao Jiang. Prior to that, I studied at Wuhan University under the supervision of Prof. Zhengquan Xu.

As a researcher in the field of multimedia forensics and security, my research is related to AI Generated Content Detection\Fake Image&Video Detection\Video Double Compression Detection\Deep learning\Machine Learning, etc. I am committed to leveraging advanced technologies to address real-world multimedia information security challenges.

Recruitment: Actively looking for self-motivated students, interns/engineers/visitors (CV/ML/Cybersecurity background, always welcome) to join us in Information Security and Intelligence Cognition Lab (ICL). If you share same/similar interests, feel free to drop me an email with your resume.
